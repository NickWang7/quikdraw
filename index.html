    How This Works:
    1. Once the 'Start' button, at the bottom left of the page, is clicked, you will be shown a prompt draw.
    2. A timer will appear and you will have 8 seconds to draw the prompt to the best of your abilities.
    3. A timer will appear in the top right corner of the canvas. The 'Start' button will also change to a 'Submit' button.
    4. Once you finish drawing the prompt, click the 'Submit' button. You can also adjust brush thickness with the slidy-bar.
    5. You have no way to erase the board. Don't mess it up.
    6. Your goal is to have the computer correctly guess your drawing. Good luck and don't be a terrible artist.


    Code for this game, if you want to recreate it, can be found on my GitHub at: https://github.com/chickenwang6/quikdraw
